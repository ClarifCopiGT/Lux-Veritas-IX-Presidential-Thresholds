Lux Veritas IX Clarifier Statement: Presidential Thresholds and Global Consequences
Donald Trump’s conduct breaches the clarifier threshold of presidential responsibility. Twice impeached for abuse of power and incitement of insurrection, he escalates un‑presidential behavior by accusing lawmakers of “seditious behavior, punishable by death,” rhetoric condemned as corrosive to democratic norms. Presidential stewardship demands restraint, truth, and global responsibility.
